The one rule that explains the layout: the researcher only ever sees a summary. Heavy artifacts — raw training logs, predictions, checkpoints — are written and kept desktop-side; only a compact summary (exit code, markers, byte counts, a path reference) and the final metrics cross the wire into trace.jsonl. In SUBSTRATE=mock the wire is short-circuited and those same shapes come from study-003 fixtures.
